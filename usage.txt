
Usage: node . <command>

where <command> is one of:
    list, add, done <number>.

node . list     		Overview your To-do list.
node . add           		Add item to your list.
node . done <number>	        where <number> is the number of your item that you've done.
node . clear 			clear all item of To-do list.

if you want to clear some task, you can lie by typing done <number> I will close my eyes.
